Our Story

Born from the tandoor, refined by time.

Ember & Spice began as a single wood-fired clay oven in a courtyard kitchen. Today we carry that same obsession forward — charring, smoking, slow-cooking — guided by chefs who trained from Lucknow to Lyon.

Every dish on our menu has a story rooted in a region, a season, or a grandmother's memory. We don't just cook food — we restore it.
